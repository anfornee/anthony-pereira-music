I grew up surrounded by music.

For as long as I can remember every part of my life has included music. I am in love with it and it's ability to tell a story. Music is a language that every person on this planet can understand and I use that to tell them something.

Personal projects will always be something that I work on but I love to partner with people that also have a passion for telling great stories. I've spent some time engineering and mixing and collaborating with other songwriters. Music can be best heard when it is attached to something that is seen. Having my music be a part of incredible visuals is one of my favorite things. Please reach out if you have a story you want to tell in anyway.
